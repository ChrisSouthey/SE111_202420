John Wick (2014): Review page

Chris Southey, February 1st, 2024


A review of John Wick (2014): John Wick is a movie follows the main character John Wick, a retired assassin enjoying life with his wife Helen. John's wife suddenly passes from an illness but leaves john with one last gift after she passes, a puppy named Daisy. In the days following his wife's death John struggles to cope, finding comfort in his new dog and his beloved 1969 Ford Mustang. While out for a drive with his dog he comes across the son of a russian mob boss who atempts to buy his car, when John declines the offer this angered the son. Later that night the son and his crew breakes into John's house, beats him up, kills his dog, and steals his car. This attack causes John to come out of retirement to get revenge for his dog and to get his car back by any means necessary.
